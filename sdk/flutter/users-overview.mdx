**Quick Reference for AI Agents & Developers**

Choose your path:
- **Retrieve Users** → [retrieve-users](/sdk/flutter/retrieve-users) - Fetch and filter user lists
- **User Presence** → [user-presence](/sdk/flutter/user-presence) - Track online/offline status
- **Block Users** → [block-users](/sdk/flutter/block-users) - Block and unblock users
- **User Management** → [user-management](/sdk/flutter/user-management) - Create, update, delete users
</Info>

The primary aim for our users functionality is to allow you to quickly retrieve and add users to CometChat.

You can begin with [user management](/sdk/flutter/user-management) to sync your users to CometChat. Once that is done, you can [retrieve users](/sdk/flutter/retrieve-users) and display them in your app.

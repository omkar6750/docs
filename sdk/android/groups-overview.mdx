Groups help your users converse together in a single space. You can have three types of groups: private, public, and password protected.

<Note>
**Available via:** SDK | [REST API](https://api-explorer.cometchat.com) | [UI Kits](/ui-kit/android/overview)
</Note>

Each group includes three kinds of users: admin, moderator, and member.

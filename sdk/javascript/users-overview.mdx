**Quick Reference for AI Agents & Developers**

- [User Management](/sdk/javascript/user-management) — Create and update users
- [Retrieve Users](/sdk/javascript/retrieve-users) — Fetch and filter user lists
- [User Presence](/sdk/javascript/user-presence) — Track online/offline status
- [Block Users](/sdk/javascript/block-users) — Block and unblock users
</Info>

The primary aim for our Users functionality is to allow you to quickly retrieve and add users to CometChat.

You can begin with [user management](/sdk/javascript/user-management) to sync your users to CometChat. Once that is done, you can [retrieve users](/sdk/javascript/retrieve-users) and display them in your app.
